Privacy Policy — Nospree

Last updated: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nospree collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the app.

Nospree is a personal organization app designed to help you track specific expenses, record daily answers, view history, receive reminders, and, when available, protect your progress with an account and backup.

Nospree is not a bank, financial institution, digital wallet, credit service, investment service, financial advisor, accounting advisor, or legal advisor.

1. Who we are

Nospree is published by Masa Labs.

Contact: nospree.app@gmail.com

2. Information we may collect or store

2.1 Account information

When you create or access an account in Nospree, we may process information such as your email address, account identifier, information required for authentication, and account status, including whether Premium features are active through a subscription or manual/beta access.

Authentication may be processed by third-party providers such as Supabase. We do not store your password in readable text.

2.2 Information entered or generated in the app

Nospree may store information that you add, configure, or generate in the app, including tracked expenses, expense names, average amounts, weekdays configured for each expense, daily answers, actual amounts entered, usage history, balances, consistency, projections and insights calculated from your data, language, currency, theme and notification preferences, onboarding state, and app settings.

This information may be stored locally on your device. If you use an account and backup, some of this information may also be stored in the cloud.

2.3 Backup data

If you use Premium features with a connected account, Nospree may save a backup of your data in the cloud to allow later restoration.

The backup may include app data such as tracked expenses, history, settings, and preferences. The backup should not include your password, session tokens, secret keys, full credit card details, internal credentials, or local test Premium state.

2.4 Purchases and subscriptions

If you subscribe to Premium features, the purchase may be processed by Google Play and/or subscription management services such as RevenueCat.

We may receive information needed to unlock or verify Premium status, such as whether a subscription is active, expired, canceled, or in a trial period. We do not receive or store your full credit card details.

2.5 Notifications

Nospree may use local notifications to remind you to record expenses, review your progress, or maintain consistency. You can enable, disable, or change notifications in the app or device settings.

2.6 Technical and diagnostic data

We may collect or receive minimal technical data needed to keep the app working, diagnose failures, prevent abuse, improve stability, and comply with security and platform requirements. This may include device type, system version, app version, error logs, and technical events related to app operation if such tools are used.

3. How we use information

We use information to operate the app, save and display your progress, calculate history and insights, manage tracked expenses and daily answers, send notifications configured by you, provide backup and restoration, authenticate your account, unlock and verify Premium features, provide support, improve stability and security, and comply with legal, regulatory, and platform obligations.

4. Legal bases

Where applicable, we process personal data based on legal grounds such as performance of a contract or service requested by you, consent when required, compliance with legal or regulatory obligations, legitimate interests such as security, abuse prevention, app improvement and support, and regular exercise of rights.

5. Sharing information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with providers required to operate the app, including Supabase for authentication, database and backup; Google Play for app distribution, purchases and subscriptions; RevenueCat for subscription management when integrated; and infrastructure, diagnostics, analytics or support providers if used in the future.

These providers process data according to their own policies and only to the extent necessary to provide their services to Nospree.

We may also share information when necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect rights, investigate abuse, respond to requests from competent authorities, or enforce our terms.

6. Storage and security

Some data may be stored locally on your device. If you use an account and backup, some data may also be stored on third-party servers used by Nospree.

We use reasonable measures to protect information, such as authentication, access control, and services with security features. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the app, maintain your account, allow backup and restoration, process Premium features, provide support, comply with legal, tax, regulatory or platform obligations, and prevent fraud, abuse or misuse.

You can delete local data from your device in the app settings. Deleting local data does not automatically delete your account or remote backup.

8. Account and data deletion

If you created a Nospree account, you may request deletion of your account and associated data through the page: Delete account and data.

When you request deletion, we may delete or anonymize data associated with your account, such as profile data, cloud backup, and account-linked app data. Some data may be retained when necessary to comply with legal, tax, regulatory, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or platform requirements.

Deleting your Nospree account does not automatically cancel a subscription processed by Google Play. To cancel a subscription, use your Google Play account subscription settings.

9. User rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights related to your personal data, including confirming whether we process your data, accessing it, correcting it, requesting anonymization, blocking or deletion, requesting portability, revoking consent, requesting information about sharing, and objecting to certain processing where applicable.

To exercise your rights, contact us at nospree.app@gmail.com.

10. Children

Nospree is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided personal data to Nospree, contact us so we can evaluate and take appropriate action.

11. International transfers

Providers used by Nospree may process or store data in other countries. By using the app, you understand that your data may be processed outside your country of residence as necessary to operate the service.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When this happens, we will update the “Last updated” date. If changes are significant, we may inform you through the app, this page, or another appropriate method.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at nospree.app@gmail.com.
